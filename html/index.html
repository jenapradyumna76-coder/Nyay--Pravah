Description

NYAY Pravah is an AI-powered legal platform designed to make justice workflows faster, simpler, and more accessible. It helps users search cases intelligently, connect with legal support, and manage legal tasks with clarity. The platform is built to reduce complexity in legal processes by combining smart search, structured guidance, and secure digital tools for judges, lawyers, and citizens.

Our goal is to accelerate legal decision-making while keeping transparency and usability at the center. From case discovery to document organization and legal aid assistance, the app provides one modern system where users can track, understand, and act on legal information efficiently.
